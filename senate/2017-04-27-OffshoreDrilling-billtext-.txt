      Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
 SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON REVISING APPROVED 20172022 OIL AND GAS LEASING PROGRAM. The Secretary of the Interior shall not revise the approved oil and gas leasing program for the period of fiscal years 2017 through 2022 prepared under section 18 of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act (43 U.S.C. 1344) and described in the document entitled ‘‘20172022 Outer Continental Shelf Oil and Gas Leasing Proposed Final Program’’ and dated November 2016.
