> To repeal the authority under the National Labor Relations
>     Act for States to enact laws prohibiting agreements requiring membership in a labor organization as a condition of employment, and for other purposes.
     Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
 SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ‘‘Protecting Workers and Improving Labor Standards Act’’.
 SEC. 2. PREEMPTING STATE RIGHT-TO-WORK LAWS. Subsection (b) of section 14 of the National Labor Relations Act (29 U.S.C. 164) is repealed.
