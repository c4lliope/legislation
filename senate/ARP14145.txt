>                     A BILL
> To amend the Endangered Species Act of 1973 to require
>     disclosure to States of the basis of determinations under
>     that Act, to ensure use of information provided by State,
>     tribal, and county governments in decisionmaking under
>     that Act, and for other purposes.
     Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
 SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ‘‘State, Tribal, and Local Species Transparency and Recovery Act’’.
 SEC. 2. ENSURING USE OF STATE, TRIBAL, AND LOCAL INFORMATION. Section 3 of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (16 U.S.C. 1532) is amended—
          (1) by redesignating paragraphs (2) through
     (10) as paragraphs (3) through (11), respectively; and
          (2) by inserting after paragraph (1) the following:
     ‘‘(2) BEST SCIENTIFIC AND COMMERCIAL DATA AVAILABLE .—The term ‘best scientific and commercial data available’ includes all relevant data submitted by a State, tribal, or county government.’’.
 SEC. 3. REQUIRING DECISIONAL TRANSPARENCY WITH AFFECTED STATES. Section 6 of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (16 U.S.C. 1535) is amended—
          (1) by striking the section designation and heading and all that follows through ‘‘In carrying out’’ in subsection (a) and inserting the following:
 ‘‘SEC. 6. COOPERATION WITH STATES.
     ‘‘(a) COOPERATION .—
          ‘‘(1)N GENERAL .—In carrying out’’; and
          (2) in subsection (a), in the second sentence, by striking ‘‘Such cooperation shall include consultation’’ and inserting the following:
      ‘‘(2) INCLUSIONS .—Cooperation under paragraph (1) shall include—
          ‘‘(A) before making a determination under section 4(a), providing to States affected by the determination all data that are the basis of the determination; and
          ‘‘(B) consultation’’.
